What's the current carbon impact of improper plastic waste disposal globally, and what are the top 3 most effective alternatives?
AI
Great question. Improper plastic disposal generates approximately 850 million metric tons of CO₂ equivalent annually — roughly 2.4% of global emissions.

The top 3 most effective alternatives are:

1. Biodegradable Polymers — PHA and PLA-based plastics decompose naturally and reduce landfill methane by up to 70%.

2. Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR) — Holding manufacturers accountable has reduced single-use plastic in 34 countries by an average of 40%.

3. Chemical Recycling — Pyrolysis and gasification converts unrecyclable plastics into fuel, recovering up to 91% of energy content.
